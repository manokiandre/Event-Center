Temple Stadium — Location & Access

Address

123 Stadium Way, Temple City, CA 90000

Coordinates: 34.1000° N, 118.0500° W

Neighborhood: Temple City Sports District, adjacent to Civic Center Plaza

Hours & Entry

  • Box Office: Mon-Fri 10:00 AM-6:00 PM; event days open until halftime
  • Gates Open: 90 minutes before event start
  • Last Entry: Until start of the 4th quarter / 2nd half (event-dependent)

Parking

  • On-Site Lots: A, B, C (credit/debit only)
  • Oversize Vehicles: Lot C (prepaid permit required)
  • Rideshare/Drop-Off: Curbside at Gate 3 loop
  • Accessible Parking: Reserved spaces in Lot A (placard required)

Transit

  • Light Rail: Temple City Station (0.3 mi, ~6-minute walk)
  • Bus Lines: 10, 22, 45 stop at Stadium Way & 3rd
  • Bike Access: Protected lane on Stadium Way; racks at Gates 1 & 4

Accessibility

  • Seating: ADA seating at concourses 101, 118, 205
  • Elevators: Near Gates 2 and 5
  • Assisted Listening: Available at Guest Services (photo ID required)
  • Service Animals: Permitted consistent with venue policy

Guest Services

  • Lost & Found: Section 120 kiosk or online form
  • First Aid: Concourse 112 and Upper 206
  • Security Screening: Walk-through magnetometers; clear-bag policy in effect

Contact

  • General Inquiries: (555) 123-4567
  • Email: info@templestadium.example
  • Event Day Hotline: (555) 987-6543
